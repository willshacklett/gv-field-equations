# Effective Action (Sketch)

## Goal
Write an effective action whose Euler–Lagrange equations yield a GV dynamical law.

## Candidate form (placeholder)

\[
S_{\text{eff}}=\int d^4x \sqrt{-g}\left[
\frac{M_P^2}{2}R
-\frac{1}{2}K(GV)\,(\nabla GV)^2
- V(GV)
+ \mathcal{L}_{\text{matter}}(g,\psi; GV)
\right]
\]

Where:
- \(K(GV)\) is a kinetic prefactor (stiffness)
- \(V(GV)\) is an effective potential (constraint energy)
- matter couplings may depend on GV (screening/decoupling)

## Outputs
From this action:
- derive GV equation of motion
- derive stress-energy contributions → \(\rho_{\text{eff}}(GV)\)
- define regimes:
  - supercritical (inflation-like)
  - undercritical (dark-energy tail)
  - metastable domains (dark-matter-like)
